INTRODUCING PARADISE BREEZE NASSAU CURIO COLLECTION BY HILTON

Nestled along the stunning Bahamian coastline, Paradise Breeze is a modern sanctuary designed to offer both hotel guests and residence owners the perfect balance of vibrant adventure and unparalleled relaxation. Surrounded by breathtaking ocean views and coastal landscapes, this hybrid resort located on West Bay St., Nassau, Bahamas, will offer 117 elegantly appointed suites and 8 exclusive penthouses.

Paradise Breeze will be home to multiple dining destinations, including a rooftop signature restaurant and a pool restaurant, as well as over 4000 square feet of versatile event space, a spectacular infinity pool, curated social lounges and engaging activities for children of all ages. For those seeking wellness and rejuvenation, the resort will offer a state-of-the-art fitness center with squash and padel courts, a yoga and relaxation garden, along with our signature hydrotherapy spa.

From its thoughtfully curated amenities to its beautiful ocean views, this beachfront resort and residences will seamlessly blend refined elegance with the vibrant spirit of the Bahamas, making every moment feel like paradise. Now selling.
